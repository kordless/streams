Note: Instance Timers

Instances may occasionally be preempted before their run timer expires. Stopped instances may be restarted at any time before their life timer runs out. Instance life timers for this accont are a maximum of 604,800 seconds or 7 days. Once a life timer has run out, the instance will be terminated and deleted, even if the run timer has time left on it.
